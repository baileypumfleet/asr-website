Welcome to ASR!

We are a self-organised and non-profit organisation who is based within the United Kingdom but provides free racing tournaments to amateur racers across the globe. We are open to anybody of any skill set across our games of racing include the F1 Racing Series, Assetto Corsa, Forza Motorsport and much more.

Getting Started

Step 1

Browse our race schedule and find a race that you'd like to participate in.

Step 2

Click the register button and enter your details to create a new account.

Step 3

You're all registered! In future, when registering for a race, you can use your existing account.
